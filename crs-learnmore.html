Community Rotating Savings (CRS)

CRS has emerged to enable people to save and borrow easily and in a hassle-free manner. It has its roots in traditional community-based rotating savings and credit associations.From the formation of group schedules & auctions to managing collections and disbursement

With CRS Money is not idle for long but changes hands rapidly, satisfying both consumption and production needs.it serves as a mechanism for saving, provides capital with little or no interest, strengthens networks and helps to accumulate assets. We charge 2% on all group savings monthly
